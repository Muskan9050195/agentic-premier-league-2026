Challenge 1 — StadiumAI

What is StadiumAI?

StadiumAI is an agentic, AI-powered stadium management and fan experience app built for large-scale cricket events like the IPL.

It uses the **Google Gemini API** to provide real-time intelligent assistance to both stadium staff and attendees — acting not just as a chatbot, but as a true **agentic assistant** that understands live context and takes action.

Features

Live Dashboard — Real-time crowd density, wait times, and zone status across 6 stadium zones
AI Chat Assistant — Powered by Gemini, answers fan queries about queues, navigation, food, and facilities in natural language
Navigation Panel — Gate-by-gate status, crowd levels, and smart facility tips
Live Alerts — Automated alerts for congestion, reopened counters, and emergency exits
Mobile-first UI — Designed for fans on their phones inside the stadium

Agentic AI Features

The Gemini-powered agent in StadiumAI:
- Understands real-time stadium context (crowd levels, wait times, gate status)
- Gives specific, actionable recommendations (e.g. "Skip Food Court A — try Section B, only 8 min wait")
- Handles natural language queries from fans without any structured commands
- Acts as a proactive assistant — not just answering questions but guiding decisions


Challenge 2 — AdaptLearn AI

What is AdaptLearn AI?

AdaptLearn AI is an intelligent personalized learning assistant powered by Google Gemini that adapts content, depth, and pace to each individual learner.

It solves a core problem in education: one-size-fits-all teaching doesn't work. AdaptLearn watches how you respond, detects whether you understood, and adjusts in real time.

 Features

Level Selection — Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced — Gemini adapts its entire teaching style
Got it / Didn't get it" buttons — AI instantly simplifies or goes deeper based on your response
Gemini-generated Quiz — Auto-generates MCQ questions with explanations to test understanding
XP & Streak System — Gamified learning to keep users motivated
Any Topic — AI, Python, Cricket Analytics, Cloud Computing, Math, and more

 Agentic AI Features

The Gemini-powered agent in AdaptLearn:
Personalizes content to the learner's exact level in real time
Detects confusion and automatically switches teaching strategy
Generates assessments on the fly without any pre-written question bank
Adapts pace — never rushes, never bores
